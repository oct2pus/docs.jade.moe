Avatar

Summary

Avatar grabs a picture of a user’s avatar. Their actual username and discord tag will display below.

Usage

Default

==>avatar

JadeBot displays a picture of your own avatar.

Arguments

==>avatar @nickname

JadeBot displays a picture of an @’d user’s avatar.
