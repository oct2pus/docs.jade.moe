Dog

Summary

Dog displays pictures of dogs, which are taken from dog.ceo. You can contribute your own dog here. You can find a full list of breeds here.

Usage

Default

==>dog

JadeBot shows a random picture of a dog.

1 Argument

==>dog generalized_breed

JadeBot will show dogs from a generalized category.

2 Arguments

==>dog specific breed

Typing a breed in full will narrow your search to only those types of dogs.

Doge

==>doge

JadeBot posts a doge.
